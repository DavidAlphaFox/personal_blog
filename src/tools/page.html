GPG public key

Here is my GPG public key. You are encouraged to download it and add to your keyring. My old RSA key was revoked: it’s still present here to allow you to import it in your keyring as a revocation certificate. If you need to send me encrypted emails or data, use the DSA key, please.

If you have no idea how to use these keys, I think you might visit the PGP site. Personally, I prefer to use a free version of PGP, GnuPG.

Note: I recently added to the DSA key a photo ID: you can effectively verify the authenticity of this key by looking at the picture. If you recognize me, it’s a beginning. The next step is to compare the key fingerprint included in the key with this one:

0×375D5B01 key: 5C86 2A6E 8D9B 1422 8357 E070 C034 5167 375D 5B01
0xE82CEA41 key: 85 43 E9 61 0A FE 76 B7 EE D5 37 18 AE C6 2A 61

The best will be to ask me in person the fingerprint.

Have fun.
